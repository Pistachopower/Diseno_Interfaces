Meet Bilbo Baggins

The Adventurer from Middle-Earth

**Bilbo Baggins** was born in the Shire, a peaceful land in Middle-Earth. Known for his unexpected journey with Thorin and Company, Bilbo's adventures have become legendary. Passionate about exploring ancient ruins and uncovering the legends of Middle-Earth, Bilbo shares his stories with followers eager to learn about his travels.
